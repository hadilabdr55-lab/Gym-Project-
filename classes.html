NeuroFlex Classes

Training Sessions

NeuroFlex offers a structured schedule of specialized training sessions designed to improve both physical strength and mental focus. Our program includes a variety of activities such as swimming, cycling, martial arts, and fitness training. Sessions are organized at different times during the week and are dedicated separately for men and women. Depending on the activity, sessions may be led by a professional trainer or practiced independently.
